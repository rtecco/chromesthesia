How does erasing or drawing something new work during the Replay mode?

Assistant2026-03-23T19:08:12.669Z

Right now, you can still draw/erase on the canvas while replay is running, but the replay schedule was built at the moment you hit Replay — it won't include anything you add or erase afterward. The new strokes get added to the painting data, but the schedule is a snapshot.

So if you erase a stroke during replay, it disappears visually but keeps playing until you stop and restart replay.

A few options:

  1. Rebuild schedule on each loop — at the loop boundary (during the 1s pause), rebuild the schedule so the next loop reflects changes
  2. Disable drawing during replay — simpler, forces a clear mode distinction
  3. Leave as-is — the painting is the "score" and replay is a playback of that score at a point in time

What feels right?
